How to Send an Email!

To send a new email, start by clicking the Compose button in the top left corner of your Gmail page


This is the Compose window, where you will be able to write your email. Click the arrows to learn more about it!

Caption Text

This is where you'll add the recipients of the email. To add a recipient, simply type their email into this box.

Caption Two

The next box is called the subject field. The subject should say what the email is about, like a title, and is usually kept brief.

Caption Three

Finally, you'll add what you want to say in your email in this box, called the body.

Caption Three
❮ ❯

Let's look at an example. In this email, the recipient is my friend Bob, and his email is bob@gmail.com. In the subject field I've put "Hi Bob!" so that he knows my email is just a friendly hello. Finally, in the body of the email, I've written a friendly message for my friend.


When you're finished with your email, press the send button.


When you press the send button, the page will display this message in the bottom left corner to let you know your email was successfully sent!
